Privacy Policy - WhatFees

Last updated: February 15, 2026

WhatFees helps users calculate costs and track sales data. We are committed to keeping your data private and secure.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account identity data from Google Sign-In (for authentication).
  • App data you enter (presets, prices, and sales entries).
  • Purchase verification status for paid features.

2. How We Use Data

  • Authenticate you and enable access to your account.
  • Store and sync your app data across sessions/devices.
  • Determine whether Pro features are enabled.

3. Data Storage

  • Data is stored locally on your device and may be synced to cloud storage when enabled.
  • Authentication and purchase checks are processed by backend services.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Google Play Billing/verification (purchase checks)
  • Cloud hosting/database providers for API and sync

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with services required to run app features listed above.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest.

7. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can remove locally stored app data by clearing app/browser storage.
  • You can view and delete cloud account data at Data Access & Deletion.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: unschoolers.contact@gmail.com
