Shared 3D Stage Overlay

Pick the motion envelope first. All three assume realistic gold-and-black dice over the real app UI, click-through by default.

A · Recommended
Idle
Overlay stays mounted with a quiet aura and faint idle die presence. On roll, dice wake up into a brief full-screen burst, then settle back.

Ambient Idle + Burst Roll

Best balance of drama, performance, and not getting in the way.

B
Always visible
A hero die or pair is always on stage, moving slowly even when idle. Rolls escalate from that persistent presence.

Persistent Hero Dice

More personality, but it permanently occupies visual attention on the page.

C
Invisible until roll
Nothing sits on stage until a match starts. Then the overlay takes over with a short cinematic dice sequence and disappears.

Event-Only Cinematic

Highest impact per roll, but weakest sense of a persistent branded stage.

Recommendation

  • Choose A if you want the overlay system to feel shared and reusable.
  • It keeps the page alive without turning every game into a constant spectacle.
  • It gives Bracket Battle a strong roll moment without forcing Wheel/Grid into the same intensity.

Watch out for

  • B risks feeling decorative and busy.
  • C is exciting but less useful as a shared long-lived overlay system.
